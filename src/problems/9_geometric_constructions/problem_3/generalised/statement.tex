Prove that any two quadrilaterals are congruent if and only if
their sides are congruent, in order,
and the segments between the midpoints of their first and third sides are congruent.

Using only straightedge and compass,
reconstruct a quadrilateral,
given segments congruent to its four sides, in order,
and a segment congruent to the segment between the midpoints of the first and third sides.
