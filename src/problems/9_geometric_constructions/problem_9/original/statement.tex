Given two segments on the plane, of lengths $a$ and $b$,
construct, using only straightedge and compass,
a segment of length $c$ such that:
\begin{equation*}
 \sqrt[4]{c \vphantom{ab} \mspace{2mu}} = \sqrt[4]{a \vphantom{bc} \mspace{2mu}} \, + \sqrt[4]{b \vphantom{ac} \mspace{2mu}}
\end{equation*}
Note: a segment of length $1$ is not given.
