Let $f : \cinterval{0}{1} \to \mathbb{R}^{+}$ be a continuous function.
Let $a, b \in \mathbb{R}^{+}$ such that $a \leq f \leq b$.
Prove that:
\begin{equation*}
  a b \int\limits_{0}^{1} {\mspace{-9mu} \frac{1}{f \paren{x}} \dd x}
  \, \leq \,
  a + b \, - \int\limits_{0}^{1} {\mspace{-9mu} f \paren{x} \dd x}
\end{equation*}
