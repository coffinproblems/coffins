Let $f : \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}$ be a monotonically increasing function.
Let $a, b \in \mathbb{R}$ with $a < b$.
Determine which points $c \in \ointerval{a}{b}$ minimize the value:
\begin{equation*}
  \int\limits_{a}^{c} {\! f\paren{x} - f\paren{a} \dd x} \, +
  \int\limits_{c}^{b} {\! f\paren{b} - f\paren{x} \dd x}
\end{equation*}
