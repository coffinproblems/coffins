Let $h_1, h_2, h_3, h_4$ be the lengths of the altitudes of a tetrahedron.
Let $O$ be an interior point of the tetrahedron.
Let $d_1, d_2, d_3, d_4$ be the distances between $O$
and the planes containing the faces of the tetrahedron.
Show that
$h_1^4 + h_2^4 + h_3^4 + h_4^4
 \geq
 2^{10} d_1 d_2 d_3 d_4$.
