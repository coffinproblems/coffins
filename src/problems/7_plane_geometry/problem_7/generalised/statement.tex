Given a triangle,
let $R$ be the radius of its circumscribed circle,
and $r$ the radius of its inscribed circle.
Determine the distance $s$ between the centers of the two circles.

Determine the set of possible values of $s$
among all triangles that have a fixed circumradius $R$;
when are the extremes achieved? \newline
(What about in arbitrary-dimensional simplex?)
