Let $ABC$ be an equilateral triangle,
and let $O$ be a point inside it.
Show that the lengths $\overline{AO}$, $\overline{BO}$, $\overline{CO}$
can be the side lengths of a triangle,
and determine the measures of the internal angles in such triangle,
in terms of $\widehat{AOB}$ and $\widehat{BOC}$.
