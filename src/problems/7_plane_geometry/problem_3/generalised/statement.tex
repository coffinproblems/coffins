Four circles on a plane are mutually tangent to each other.
The points of tangency are all distinct.
Three of the circles have collinear centers.
Determine the distance between the center of fourth circle
and the line through the centers of the others,
in terms of the radius of the fourth circle. \newline
(There are two cases: one for internal tangency and one for external tangency)
\newline (What about for spheres in higher dimension?)
