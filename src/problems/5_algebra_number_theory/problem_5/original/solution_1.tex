The numbers $a = \sqrt{2}$ and $b = 2 \log_2\paren{3}$ are irrational,
and $a^b = 3$ is rational.

The numbers $a = \sqrt{2}$ and $b = \log_2\paren{3}$ are irrational,
and $a^b = \sqrt{3}$ is irrational.

(The numbers $\log_2\paren{3}$ and $2 \log_2\paren{3}$ are irrational
because no power of $2$ is a power of $3$,
with non-zero integer exponents).
