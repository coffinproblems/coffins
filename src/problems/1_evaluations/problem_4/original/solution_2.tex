$10^{209} \leq 125^{100} < 10^{210}$.

(since $5^5 = 3125$, and $3 \cdot 2^{10} = 3 \cdot 1024 = 3072$).
Squaring yields:
$5^{10}
 > 9 \cdot 2^{20}
 > 8 \cdot 2^{20}

Multiplying by $5^{23}$ gives:
$125^{11}

Raising to the power of $9$ gives

 > 10^{9 \cdot 23}

Finally,
$125^{100}
 = 125 \cdot 125^{99}
 > 100 \cdot 125^{99}
